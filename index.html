Meet Rachel Lansert-Langa

Founder & Creative Director of Shelly Interiors

Rachel Lansert-Langa is a passionate and visionary entrepreneur who founded Shelly Interiors in 2021. With an innate sense of style and a keen eye for detail, she has quickly established herself as a rising voice in the South African interior design scene. Her signature approach blends sophistication, practicality, and warmth — creating spaces that not only look beautiful, but also feel personal and purposeful.

As an ambivert, Rachel brings both creativity and professionalism to every project. Her ability to connect with clients and understand their unique lifestyles allows her to deliver bespoke interiors that truly reflect their needs and personalities. From cozy homes to modern commercial spaces, she transforms ordinary environments into inspired living experiences.

Rachel's growing influence in the industry has earned her recognition in Bona Digital Magazine for 2025’s Autumn Makeover Trends, a radio feature on Lokuhle FM, and an invitation to speak at a prestigious business conference. These milestones reflect her commitment to excellence and her impact as both a designer and a businesswoman.

At the heart of Shelly Interiors is Rachel's belief that great design should enhance how we live and feel in our spaces. Her studio offers a full suite of services including interior design, décor, space planning, 3D rendering, and project management. With a talented team and trusted network of suppliers, Shelly Interiors delivers personalized, stress-free experiences — turning vision into reality from concept to completion.

Whether you're starting fresh or reimagining a familiar space, Rachel and her team are ready to help you bring your dream environment to life — beautifully and seamlessly.
